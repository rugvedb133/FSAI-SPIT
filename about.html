About Us

About us

Fire & Security Association of India (FSAI) is a non-profit organization established in 2002 representing the Fire Protection, Life Safety, Security, Building Automation, Loss Prevention and Risk Management domains.

Its primary aims and objectives are to:

  • Foster a spirit of safe living among all citizens of India and inculcate a proactive mind-set towards safety and security at all times.
  • Promote and advance the use of fire safety and security systems.
  • Develop fire safety and security engineering education and awareness.
  • Maintain high ethical standards amongst its members.
FSAI aims to work closely with the Government and all other stakeholders to enable the Indian Fire and Security industry to reach global pre-eminence with better regulatory framework. FSAI was incorporated as a society with Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Rules 1975 (Tamil Nadu Act of 1975) bearing Registration No.326/2002.

FSAI has 8500+ Indian and Global members which include Professionals and Student Members, as well as leading corporations. FSAI has 24 divisional chapters, which further house many student chapters.

Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan's Sardar Patel Institute of Technology (SPIT) is an autonomous un-aided Research and Engineering Institute affiliated to University of Mumbai. The institute is located in 47 acres of green campus at Andheri (W), the fastest growing suburb of Mumbai.

In 1957, the Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan conceived the idea of establishing an engineering college in Mumbai. Sardar Patel College of Engineering was inaugurated on 19th August 1962. In 1995, self-financed engineering courses were added to it and it functioned as Sardar Patel College of Engineering (Unaided-wing) conducting Electronics Engineering, Computer Engineering and Information Technology Courses.

In 2005, Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan’s Sardar Patel College of Engineering, Unaided Wing was established as a separate institute in its new building under the name and style of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan’s Sardar Patel Institute of Technology and is affiliated to Mumbai University. In 2017, it was conferred Autonomous status by Mumbai University and thus became the youngest institute affiliated to Mumbai University to achieve this.

In 2017, a student chapter of FSAI was established in S.P.I.T. with the help of the FSAI Mumbai Chapter. A course on Fire Safety and Security was introduced into the cirriculum, which is conducted a series of guest lectures and demonstrations by industry professionals. The Student Chapter helps also these initiatives while also conducting extracirricular activities for Internal and External students.
